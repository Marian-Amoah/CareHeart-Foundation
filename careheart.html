ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

The Foundation has been able to thrive on the shoulders of these abled noble executives. The main brain behind this foundation is Miss. Alberta Gyasi-Agyeman together with Miss. Marian Boye-Amoah, Miss. Vera Adusei and Miss. Maria Boye-Amoah brought to light this idea. With time, Miss. Mauduana Ama-Menya, Miss. Boakyewaa, Miss. Franscica Kumi, Mister. Michael and Mister. Felix joined the four above as executives to help in the management of the foundation.

OUR MISSION

This is the main program of the union held every Sunday to provide an atmosphere for to students to worship God and fellowship with the brethren. You are always welcome to fellowship with us on every sunday at the commonwealth hall chapel. The time is 10am.

OUR VISION

These meetings provide an environment for members of the union to meet and fellowship as a family in smaller cells based on their respective halls.It comes off every Monday at various scheduled places and times.This enhances spiritual growth among members of the union.

OUR POLICIES

This is the union’s mid-week service held every Tuesday at exactly 6:15pm at the Akuafo hall chapel to evoke the power of God.we meet as a body to revitalize our spiritual lives with the word.Come and be a part of this power packed service

OUR MOTTO

This is an evening prayer meeting organised by the union to create an atmosphere the union to communicate with God as a church.It comes off every Wednesday from 7:30pm to 9:30pm adjacent the University guest.Join us and let’s pray together as a church in the body of Christ.
